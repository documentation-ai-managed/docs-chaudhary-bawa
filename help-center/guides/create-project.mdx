## Introduction

This guide shows how to create a project in Chaudhary Bawa.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://chaudharybawa.com/login` and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    Click on "Projects" in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click "New Project", enter a name, and click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Adding Docs">
    Select your new project and begin adding or organizing your documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
