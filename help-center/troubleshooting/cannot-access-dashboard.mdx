---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Resolve dashboard login or access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to log in
- Dashboard page not loading
- Error: "Access Denied"

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your credentials">
    Ensure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try clearing your cache and refreshing the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Use the "Forgot password" link if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support if issues persist">
    Email support@chaudharybawa.com with details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
