## Overview

To get started, sign up for a Chaudhary Bawa account and create your first project in the dashboard.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://chaudharybawa.com/signup`
2. Register with your email
3. Confirm your email address
4. Log in to the dashboard
5. Click "New Project" and follow the prompts

## Next

You can now organize, edit, and manage your project documentation online.
